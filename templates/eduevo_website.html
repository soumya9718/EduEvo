FAQ'S SECTION

Some Common FAQ's

Get answers to your questions and learn about our platform

Is EduEvo safe for students?

Yes. EduEvo only uses publicly available YouTube content and keeps your notes, history, and progress private in your account.

Do I need to pay to try EduEvo?

No. The main features are completely free. Paid plans will come later, but they’re optional.

What if my teacher isn’t available on YouTube?

EduEvo works with teachers who upload publicly. If your teacher isn’t on YouTube, you can select another teacher until his channel is added.

Will EduEvo show ads while I'm studying?

EduEvo does not show video ads or anything disruptive. Only occasional small banner ads may appear — placed carefully so they never distract your study flow.

Do I need to log in every time?

No. EduEvo keeps you signed in on your device so you can continue instantly. You only re-login if you clear your browser or switch devices.
